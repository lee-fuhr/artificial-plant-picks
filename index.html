How we choose our picks

We don’t claim to have unboxed every fake fern on the market. Instead, we sift through long-term owner reviews on major retail sites, forum discussions among professional interior designers, and published evaluations from established home-decor outlets. The key metrics we track are leaf realism (no obvious plastic shine or visible seam lines), stem flexibility for reshaping, and how well the plant holds up after months of indirect light and dusting. The biggest failure points in artificial plants are flimsy pots that tip over, colors that fade or take on a dusty gray cast, and leaves that fall off or bend awkwardly after handling. We prioritize picks that avoid these common pitfalls based on consistent feedback from owners who have had them in place for a year or more. We also note whether a plant looks good from multiple angles and whether its size matches its listed dimensions in real homes.
